Vikunja is an open-source, self-hostable to-do app designed to help you organize tasks, collaborate with teams, and view projects in multiple formats.

Features:
• Multiple project views: List, Gantt chart, Table, and Kanban board
• Full task management: Create, organize, and assign tasks with priorities, labels, and due dates
• Team collaboration: Share projects and collaborate with other users in real-time
• Self-hosted: Run Vikunja on your own server and keep full control of your data
• Open Source: Community-driven development with transparency and privacy-first design
• Cross-platform: Available on Android, iOS, and web browsers

Privacy First:
Vikunja is designed with privacy in mind. You can self-host the entire application on your own infrastructure or use Vikunja Cloud. Either way, your data remains yours.

Project Management:
• Create unlimited projects and tasks
• Organize with customizable labels and filters
• Track progress with task status and priorities
• Set deadlines and reminders
• Add comments and attachments to tasks

Collaboration:
• Share projects with team members
• Assign tasks to users
• Real-time updates and synchronization
• User roles and permissions

Requirements:
• Access to a Vikunja server (self-hosted or Vikunja Cloud)
• Android 7.0 or higher
• Internet connection

Learn More:
Visit https://vikunja.io to learn more about Vikunja and how to set up your own instance.

Note:
This app requires a Vikunja server to function. If you don't have one already, you can self-host it or use Vikunja Cloud.
